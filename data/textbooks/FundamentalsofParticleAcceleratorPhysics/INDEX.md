## Chapters

### ch01_special_relativity.md (lines 1–436 of source)
Covers Lorentz-Fitzgerald transformations, relativistic kinematics (length contraction, time dilation, velocity composition), relativistic dynamics (4-vectors, momentum, mass-energy equivalence), wave-particle duality, Doppler effect, angular collimation, and relativistic electromagnetic fields. Foundation for understanding beam behavior at relativistic energies.
**Sections index**: ch01_special_relativity_SECTIONS.md

### ch02_accelerator_types.md (lines 437–611 of source)
Classification of particle accelerators by geometry, technology, and energy. Covers electrostatic accelerators (Cockcroft-Walton, Van de Graaff, Tandem) and electrodynamic accelerators (Wideroe drift tube linac, cyclotron, synchro-cyclotron, betatron). Introduces weak focusing and vertical stability.
**Sections index**: ch02_accelerator_types_SECTIONS.md

### ch03_rf_cavities.md (lines 612–925 of source)
RF cavity physics: acceleration theorem, pill-box cavity modes (TM/TE), periodic structures (travelling wave, standing wave), synchronous phase, transit time factor, RLC circuit model, constant impedance and constant gradient structures, superconducting vs normal-conducting comparison.
**Sections index**: ch03_rf_cavities_SECTIONS.md

### ch04_high_energy_accelerators.md (lines 926–2072 of source)
Core chapter covering synchrotrons and storage rings. Longitudinal dynamics: phase stability, adiabatic damping, momentum compaction, transition energy, RF acceptance, energy ramp. Transverse dynamics: multipolar fields, quadrupoles, strong focusing, FODO lattice, transfer matrices, Hill's equation, betatron functions, Floquet's theorem, Courant-Snyder invariant, phase space ellipse, dispersion. Beam envelope: statistical emittance, beam matrix, normalized emittance, brightness.
**Sections index**: ch04_high_energy_accelerators_SECTIONS.md

### ch05_hamiltonian_dynamics.md (lines 2073–2414 of source)
Hamiltonian formulation of beam dynamics: Lagrange's and Hamilton's equations, single particle Hamiltonian in accelerator coordinates, Hill's equation derivation. Liouville's theorem, Vlasov's equation, emittance conservation. Poincare-Cartan invariants, eigen-emittance, flat/round beam transformation, magnetic bunch compression.
**Sections index**: ch05_hamiltonian_dynamics_SECTIONS.md

### ch06_perturbations_linear_optics.md (lines 2415–2649 of source)
Four perturbations to linear optics: orbit distortion (single pass and closed orbit, amplification factor), tune resonances (integer, half-integer, sum/difference resonances, sextupole resonances), linear chromaticity (natural chromaticity, sextupole correction), and multi-bend lattice chromaticity considerations.
**Sections index**: ch06_perturbations_linear_optics_SECTIONS.md

### ch07_synchrotron_radiation.md (lines 2650–2892 of source)
Synchrotron radiation fundamentals: Lienard-Wiechert retarded potentials, Larmor's formula, Schwinger's formula, radiated power in synchrotrons. Angular distribution for longitudinal and centripetal acceleration. Spectral distribution: critical frequency, universal function, intensity, and polarization.
**Sections index**: ch07_synchrotron_radiation_SECTIONS.md

### ch08_radiation_damping.md (lines 2893–3312 of source)
Radiation damping and quantum excitation in storage rings: damping of synchrotron and betatron oscillations, Robinson's theorem, radiation integrals, equilibrium emittance of multi-bend lattices, Vlasov-Fokker-Planck equation. Beam lifetime: quantum lifetime, dynamic aperture, overvoltage/energy acceptance, residual gas interactions, Touschek lifetime.
**Sections index**: ch08_radiation_damping_SECTIONS.md

### ch09_perturbations_distribution.md (lines 3313–3552 of source)
Perturbations to particle distribution: synchro-betatron excitation (emittance growth from dispersive RF), intrabeam scattering (storage rings and linacs), and collective effects (wakefields, impedances, classification of instabilities, CSR, resistive wall, parasitic loss, Robinson's instability).
**Sections index**: ch09_perturbations_distribution_SECTIONS.md

### ch10_light_sources.md (lines 3553–4325 of source)
Light sources: brilliance and diffraction limit, coherence (correlation functions, transverse/longitudinal coherence, degeneracy parameter). Undulator radiation: central wavelength, spectral width, harmonics, dipole/wiggler/undulator comparison. Inverse Compton scattering and Thomson back-scattering. Free-electron lasers: resonance condition, pendulum equation, low/high gain regimes, Pierce's parameter, electron beam quality requirements.
**Sections index**: ch10_light_sources_SECTIONS.md

### ch11_colliders.md (lines 4326–4463 of source)
Collider physics: luminosity definition and optimization, beam lifetime vs run time, crossing angle geometry, hourglass effect, luminosity of Compton sources. Beam-beam effects: tune shift parameter, beam-beam lifetime.
**Sections index**: ch11_colliders_SECTIONS.md
